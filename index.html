About Us

Anyone can code...

Fear No Code was created to provide an environment where anyone who has ever thought about learning how to code, can with confidence and support.

Our founders are self taught and have spent countless hours searching the best resources and building projects.

Now, it's time to share that same information with you! With thousands of tech jobs going unfilled each year, and the number's rising, Fear No Code aims to break the barrier's of tech and bridge the gap between diversity in the industry while building confidence in future coder's.
